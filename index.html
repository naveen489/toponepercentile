About Top One Percentile

Where Potential Meets Excellence

Top One Percentile (TOP) is a premier coaching institute dedicated to transforming students into top achievers. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between school academics and competitive exam preparation, we offer a unique blend of concept-based learning and personalized mentoring.

At TOP, every student receives individual attention in small batch sizes, ensuring no one is left behind. Our regular assessments, doubt-clearing sessions, and parent update meetings create a holistic ecosystem for academic success.
