📖 Biography

Welcome to Top One Percentile (TOP) — an institute built on the belief that every student carries within them the potential to achieve extraordinary results. I am the lead educator and founder of this institute, and my journey as a teacher has been driven by one simple yet powerful conviction: great teaching transforms lives.

With over 8 years of dedicated experience in coaching students for competitive examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, NTSE, and Board Exams (CBSE/ICSE/State Boards), I have had the privilege of witnessing hundreds of students go from average performers to top achievers. My approach is rooted in conceptual clarity, a thorough understanding of exam patterns, and building genuine academic confidence in each student.

I founded TOP with the vision of creating an institute where students are not just given notes and told to memorize — but where they understand the why behind every concept. I design my classes to bridge the gap between school academics and competitive exam demands, ensuring students are equally strong in boards and entrance exams.

My teaching style is adaptive — I study each student's strengths, learning pace, and weaknesses, then tailor my guidance accordingly. I strongly believe in small batch sizes because every student deserves individual attention. Regular assessments, doubt-clearing sessions, and parent progress updates are the pillars of my teaching methodology. I am not just your tutor — I am your academic mentor, motivator, and guide throughout your journey.
